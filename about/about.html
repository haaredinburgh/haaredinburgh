About Haar

Who we are.

Haar is Edinburgh's free event directory — the events map of the city. A calendar of everything happening across Edinburgh, in one place. Browse by date, type, or area. Live music, club nights, comedy, arts, and community events. Every listing clicks straight through to the event's own page. We drive the traffic, you keep it.

Our ethos.

No advertisers, no agenda. Our aim is to be part of a wider positive movement for the city's arts and community. Free to list, free to browse. Community before profit.
